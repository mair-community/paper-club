# MAIR Paper Club

MAIR Paper Club is a regular community activity where members present and discuss AI papers in an accessible and collaborative way. The goal is to make research easier to understand, encourage critical discussion, and help members stay up to date with global AI developments.

Sessions are usually presented in Darija, making advanced AI topics more approachable for members in Morocco and the wider Maghreb region. Each session focuses on understanding the core ideas of a paper, its technical contribution, its limitations, and its potential relevance to research, industry, and the local AI ecosystem.
